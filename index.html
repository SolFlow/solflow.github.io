Inspiration


The inspiration comes from a field trip, imagine you, being a children, travels with your family to a large field of sunflowers, where all of the flowers smiles at you. The color of yellow lights up the whole sky and make you smile happily. We are trying to preserve such feeling even when you are back at home. Looking at solflower, you feel like your soul has been lightened to a brighter day.

So we have dedicated to make the thing artistic, humanistic and fantastic, you can sit on a couch, look at the sol flower, and let your mind goes on a virtual trip to one of the most beautiful corner in the earth — The Sunflower Garden
